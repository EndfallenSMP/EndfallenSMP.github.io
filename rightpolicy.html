EndfallenSMP Internal Rights Policy

1. Ownership and Contribution

All original creations (e.g., characters, builds, story arcs) made by a user on EndfallenSMP remain their intellectual property. However, by participating in the server, contributors grant EndfallenSMP the perpetual, non-exclusive right to use, distribute, and showcase submitted content for server development, lore, events, and promotional material.

2. Lore Integrity

Lore contributions become part of the shared server universe. Major modifications, rewrites, or removals of lore-affecting elements require approval from the Lore Council or server administrators.

3. Administrative Roles

Staff positions grant limited administrative rights. These do not override creative ownership unless the role was involved in co-creation. Abuse of authority (e.g., forced lore changes, credit theft) will result in disciplinary action.

4. Content Sharing and Publishing

  • Videos, screenshots, or any other media published externally must credit EndfallenSMP if showcasing server content.
  • Monetized content using server-created lore or characters must disclose the server name and origin.

5. Dispute Resolution

Any creative disputes (character arcs, build ownership, lore branching) should first be reported to the server’s Lore Council. If unresolved, the server Owner has the final say.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may evolve with the server. All changes will be communicated via announcements. Continued participation implies acceptance of new terms.

7. Final Clause

EndfallenSMP respects and protects the creativity of its members. This document aims to ensure fairness, recognition, and collaboration across all content and development efforts.
