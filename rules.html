🚨 Consequences

Severe violations—such as hacking, doxxing, major griefing, discrimination, or pedophilic actions—may result in an immediate permanent ban.

Persistent or targeted misconduct after punishment may escalate to permanent bans in the following cases:

a) Harassment of staff or admins via direct messages.

b) Providing false information to manipulate or shorten your punishment.

c) DMing other members to complain about the punishment being “unfair.”
