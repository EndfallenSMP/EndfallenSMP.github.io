What is EndfallenSMP?

EndfallenSMP is a Minecraft: Bedrock Edition roleplay-focused server powered by custom-made addons.

We value rich storytelling and character-driven narratives. It’s the perfect place to begin your roleplay adventure!

Participate in events, connect with other players, and explore your character’s potential through immersive experiences.

The current season, "Founder's Demise", follows the story of the Archiver, transported to the Ruthia Region on another planet, slowly descending into madness.

Meanwhile, The Founder, Ireatnifis, seeks entry into this world to continue his torment—as he always has.
